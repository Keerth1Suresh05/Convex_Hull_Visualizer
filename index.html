Graham Scan Algorithm

1. Find P₀ (lowest Y)
2. Polar sort (cross product)
3. Conter clockwise scan (while cross ≤ 0: pop)

Divide and Conquer Approach

Divide

Find the starting point P₀ (lowest Y value). Then sort all other points by their polar angle relative to P₀.

Conquer

Scan the sorted points and build the hull. Use the CCW test to detect turns. Remove points that create a clockwise turn.

Combine

The remaining points in the stack form the convex hull boundary. Connecting them creates the final polygon.
